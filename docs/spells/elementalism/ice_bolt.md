# Ice Bolt

## Overview

Ice Bolt launches a bolt of freezing ice at the target, dealing cold damage and potentially slowing the enemy. This fundamental cold spell provides reliable single-target damage with crowd control potential.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Projectile
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Cold Damage: 150% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Cold
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Conditions:**
- Slow Chance: 35%
- Slow Duration: 6 seconds
- Slow Effect: -30% movement speed

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual ice trail
- Freezing visual effect
- Can stack with other slows

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Ice gathering in hands
- Bolt formation
- Release animation

**Projectile:**
- Ice bolt with trail
- Glowing blue/white sphere
- Frost particles

**Impact:**
- Ice explosion
- Freezing effect (if slow applied)
- Cold visual on target

**Sound Effects:**
- Ice gathering sound
- Projectile whoosh
- Ice impact sound
- Freezing sound (if slow)

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Single target cold damage
- Applying slow conditions
- Consistent magical DPS
- Crowd control

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on enemies weak to cold
- Combine with other cold spells
- Use for kiting with slow
- Stack slows for maximum effect

**Elemental Synergy:**
- Combines with Frost Nova
- Works with Blizzard
- Fire and ice combination
- Enhanced by Elemental Mastery

## Upgrades

**Level 2:** +10% damage, +5% slow chance
**Level 3:** +20% damage, +10% slow chance
**Level 4:** +30% damage, +15% slow chance
**Level 5:** +40% damage, +20% slow chance, +1s slow duration
