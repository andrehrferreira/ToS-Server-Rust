# Meteor Strike

## Overview

Meteor Strike calls down a massive meteor from the sky, dealing devastating fire damage to a large area. This rare ability provides massive area damage, making it one of the most powerful area-of-effect attacks in the elementalism skillset.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 25 units
**Area Radius:** 8 units
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 90 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Fire Damage: 300% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Fire
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Large area coverage

**Conditions:**
- Burn Chance: 50%
- Burn Duration: 10 seconds
- Burn Damage: 12% of Magic Power per second

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual meteor from sky
- Area denial effect
- High total damage potential
- Impressive visual effect

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Massive area damage
- Clearing large groups
- Boss encounters with adds
- Area denial

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on grouped enemies
- Target center of formations
- Use after crowd control
- Combine with other fire spells

**Fire Synergy:**
- Combines with Fireball
- Works with Flame Wave
- Enhanced by Elemental Mastery
- Maximum fire damage potential
