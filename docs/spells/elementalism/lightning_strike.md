# Lightning Strike

## Overview

Lightning Strike calls down a bolt of lightning from the sky, dealing energy damage and potentially stunning the target. This fundamental lightning spell provides reliable damage with crowd control potential.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 22 units
**Cast Time:** 1.2 seconds
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 28 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Energy Damage: 160% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Energy/Lightning
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Conditions:**
- Stun Chance: 25%
- Stun Duration: 1.5 seconds
- Interrupts enemy actions

**Additional Effects:**
- Instant hit (no projectile travel)
- Visual lightning bolt from sky
- Can chain to nearby enemies (10% chance)
