# Armageddon

## Overview

Armageddon is the ultimate elementalism ability, unleashing the full power of all elements in a cataclysmic explosion. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of elemental mastery, dealing catastrophic damage to all enemies in a massive area.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 30 units
**Area Radius:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** 4.5 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 200 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Fire Damage: 200% of Magic Power
- Cold Damage: 200% of Magic Power
- Energy Damage: 200% of Magic Power
- Total Damage: 600% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: All Elements
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Conditions:**
- Burn Duration: 15 seconds (50% chance)
- Freeze Duration: 3 seconds (30% chance)
- Stun Duration: 2 seconds (25% chance)
- Multiple conditions possible

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 95% while channeling
- Visual apocalypse effect
- Ultimate area damage
