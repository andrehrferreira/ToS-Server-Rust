# Frost Nova

## Overview

Frost Nova unleashes a burst of freezing cold energy in all directions, dealing cold damage and slowing all nearby enemies. This area-of-effect ability provides both damage and crowd control, essential for group combat.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (6 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Cold Damage: 120% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Cold
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Hits all enemies in radius

**Conditions:**
- Slow Duration: 8 seconds
- Slow Effect: -40% movement speed
- Applies to all enemies hit
- Cannot be resisted

**Additional Effects:**
- Instant area effect
- Visual nova burst
- Effective crowd control
- Area denial
