# Flame Wave

## Overview

Flame Wave unleashes a wave of fire in a direction, dealing fire damage to all enemies in its path. This directional area attack provides excellent area damage and is perfect for hitting multiple enemies in a line.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Camera Direction
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** 18 units (wave travels)
**Wave Width:** 4 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Fire Damage: 130% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Fire
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Hits all enemies in wave path

**Conditions:**
- Burn Chance: 30%
- Burn Duration: 6 seconds
- Burn Damage: 8% of Magic Power per second

**Additional Effects:**
- Directional wave attack
- Visual fire wave
- Area coverage
- Burn application

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Directional area damage
- Hitting multiple enemies
- Fire damage builds
- Area control

**Combat Tips:**
- Position for optimal wave path
- Use on grouped enemies
- Combine with other fire spells
- Use for area denial

**Fire Synergy:**
- Combines with Fireball
- Works with Meteor Strike
- Enhanced by Elemental Mastery
- Fire build synergy
