# Thunderstorm

## Overview

Thunderstorm creates a storm of lightning that strikes random enemies in a large area, dealing energy damage and potentially stunning targets. This rare ability provides area damage with crowd control potential.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 25 units
**Area Radius:** 12 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 85 Mana
**Duration:** 6 seconds

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Energy Damage: 120% of Magic Power per strike
- Number of Strikes: 8-12 (random, over duration)
- Total Damage: 960-1440% of Magic Power (maximum)
- Damage Type: Energy/Lightning
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per strike)

**Conditions:**
- Stun Chance: 20% per strike
- Stun Duration: 1.0 seconds
- Multiple stuns possible
- Interrupts enemy actions

**Additional Effects:**
- Random strikes in area
- Area coverage
- High total damage potential
- Crowd control potential
