# Fireball

## Overview

Fireball launches a blazing sphere of fire at the target, exploding on impact and potentially igniting enemies with burning damage over time. This fundamental fire spell provides reliable single-target damage with a chance to apply burn conditions.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Projectile
**Target Type:** Enemy or Ground Location
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Fire Damage: 150% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Fire
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Explosion Radius: 2 units (if hits ground)

**Conditions:**
- Burn Chance: 30%
- Burn Duration: 5 seconds
- Burn Damage: 10% of Magic Power per second

**Additional Effects:**
- Explodes on impact
- Can hit multiple enemies in explosion radius
- Visual fire trail

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Fire gathering in hands
- Sphere formation
- Release animation

**Projectile:**
- Fireball with trail effect
- Glowing orange/red sphere
- Sparks and embers

**Impact:**
- Explosion effect
- Fire burst
- Burn visual on affected targets

**Sound Effects:**
- Fire gathering sound
- Projectile whoosh
- Explosion sound
- Burn crackling sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Single target fire damage
- Applying burn conditions
- Area damage on grouped enemies
- Consistent magical DPS

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on enemies weak to fire
- Combine with other fire spells for burn stacking
- Aim at ground near enemies for area damage
- Use from safe distance

**Elemental Synergy:**
- Burn stacks with other fire spells
- Fire damage increased by Elemental Mastery
- Combines well with Flame Wave for area control

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base damage (150% Magic Power), 30% burn chance
**Level 2:** +10% damage, +5% burn chance
**Level 3:** +20% damage, +10% burn chance
**Level 4:** +30% damage, +15% burn chance
**Level 5:** +40% damage, +20% burn chance, +1s burn duration
