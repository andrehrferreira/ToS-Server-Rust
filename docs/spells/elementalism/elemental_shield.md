# Elemental Shield

## Overview

Elemental Shield creates a protective barrier of elemental energy that reduces incoming damage. This defensive ability provides survivability for mages, essential for surviving in combat.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Defense:**
- Damage Reduction: +25%
- Magic Resistance: +30%
- Applies to all damage types
- Stacks with other defensive abilities

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual shield effect
- Cannot be dispelled
- High survivability increase
- Essential for mage builds
