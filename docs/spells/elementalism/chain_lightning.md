# Chain Lightning

## Overview

Chain Lightning launches a bolt of lightning that bounces between multiple enemies, dealing energy damage to each target. This bouncing attack excels at dealing damage to multiple enemies, making it effective against groups.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target (Bouncing)
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units (initial)
**Bounce Range:** 8 units between targets
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Energy Damage: 140% of Magic Power (first target)
- Damage Reduction: 15% per bounce
- Maximum Bounces: 5 enemies
- Damage Type: Energy/Lightning
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per bounce)

**Bouncing:**
- Automatically targets nearest enemy
- Bounces between enemies
- Visual chain effect
- Effective against groups

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual lightning chain
- Multiple hits
- Group damage ability
- High total damage potential

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Hitting multiple enemies
- Group damage
- Chain damage builds
- Area damage alternative

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on grouped enemies
- Target center enemy for maximum bounces
- Combine with other lightning spells
- Use for group combat

**Lightning Synergy:**
- Combines with Lightning Strike
