# Elemental Mastery

## Overview

Elemental Mastery enhances the caster's connection to the elements, significantly increasing all elemental damage dealt. This rare buff ability maximizes the effectiveness of all elemental spells, essential for elemental mage builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Mana
**Duration:** 45 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Elemental Damage Increase: +40%
- Applies to Fire, Cold, and Energy damage
- Critical Strike Chance: +20% for elemental spells
- Cannot be dispelled

**Additional Effects:**
