# Blizzard

## Overview

Blizzard creates a massive snowstorm that continuously deals cold damage to all enemies in a large area. This rare ability provides sustained area damage over time, perfect for area control and group combat.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 25 units
**Area Radius:** 10 units
**Cast Time:** 2.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 50 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 100 Mana
**Duration:** 8 seconds

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Cold Damage: 80% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 640% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Cold
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per tick)

**Conditions:**
- Slow Duration: Continuous (while in area)
- Slow Effect: -50% movement speed
- Applies to all enemies in area
- Cannot be resisted

**Additional Effects:**
- Continuous damage over time
- Area denial effect
- High total damage potential
- Effective crowd control
