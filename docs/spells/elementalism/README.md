# Elementalism Skillset

## Overview

Elementalism focuses on devastating elemental magic, unleashing the raw power of fire, ice, lightning, and other elements. This skillset provides powerful area-of-effect damage, single-target nukes, and elemental conditions. Perfect for players who prefer high-damage magical combat.

## Skillset Theme

**Combat Style:** Magical DPS
**Primary Role:** Damage Dealer
**Secondary Role:** Crowd Control
**Resource:** Mana

### Common Spells (7)

1. **[Fireball](./fireball.md)** - Fire projectile with burn
2. **[Ice Bolt](./ice_bolt.md)** - Cold projectile with slow

4. **[Frost Nova](./frost_nova.md)** - Area cold damage and slow

7. **[Elemental Shield](./elemental_shield.md)** - Damage reduction buff

### Ancient Spell (1)

## Synergies

**Good Combinations:**
- **Auraweaving**: Mana regeneration and mobility
- **Occultism**: Debuffs and crowd control
- **Lifebinding**: Self-healing for sustain

**Playstyle:**
- High burst damage
- Area damage focus
- Elemental condition application
- Mana management critical
