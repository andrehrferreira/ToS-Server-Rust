# Berserker Rage

## Overview

Berserker Rage unleashes the warrior's inner fury, providing a massive increase to damage dealt at the cost of defense. This rare ability transforms the warrior into a devastating damage dealer, perfect for DPS builds and burst damage phases.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Stamina
**Duration:** 15 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Damage Increase: +50%
- Attack Speed Increase: +40%
- Critical Strike Chance: +30%
- Applies to all attacks and abilities

**Penalty:**
- Damage Taken Increase: +20%
- Defense Reduction: -30%
- High risk, high reward

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual rage effect
- Cannot be dispelled
- Stacks with Battle Frenzy
- Maximum DPS increase
