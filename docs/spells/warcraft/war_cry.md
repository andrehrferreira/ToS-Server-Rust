# War Cry

## Overview

War Cry unleashes a powerful battle cry that buffs all nearby allies and debuffs all nearby enemies. This rare group support ability provides both offensive and defensive benefits, making it essential for group content.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies and Enemies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Stamina
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Ally Buff:**
- Attack Power Increase: +20%
- Defense Increase: +15%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled

**Enemy Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -15%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -20%
- Applies to all enemies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled by enemies

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual cry effect
- Area coverage
- Group support ability
- High utility
