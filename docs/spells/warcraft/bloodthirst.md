# Bloodthirst

## Overview

Bloodthirst channels life force from the target, dealing damage and restoring health to the caster. This rare life-steal ability provides both damage and survivability, making it essential for solo play and sustained combat.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 180% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Life Steal:**
- Health Restored: 50% of damage dealt
- Can overheal (temporary health)
- Visual life steal effect
- High survivability

**Additional Effects:**
