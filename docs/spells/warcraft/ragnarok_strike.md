# Ragnarok Strike

## Overview

Ragnarok Strike is the ultimate warcraft ability, channeling apocalyptic power into a single devastating strike that deals catastrophic damage to the target. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of melee combat mastery, capable of eliminating even the strongest enemies.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 100 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 800% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (300% critical damage)
- Ignores 75% of target's armor
- Can execute enemies below 30% health (instant kill)

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Cannot be interrupted once started
- Movement speed reduced by 90% while channeling
- Visual power gathering

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual apocalypse effect
- High single-target damage
- Execute potential
- Ultimate damage ability
