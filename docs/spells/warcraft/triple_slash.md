# Triple Slash

## Overview

Triple Slash executes three rapid consecutive strikes with the weapon, dealing increasing damage with each hit. This combo ability provides high damage output and is a fundamental melee attack for warcraft builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- First Strike: 80% of Attack Power
- Second Strike: 100% of Attack Power
- Third Strike: 120% of Attack Power
- Total Damage: 300% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (each strike separately)

**Combo:**
- Must hit all three strikes
- Strikes execute in sequence
- Cannot be interrupted mid-combo
- Visual combo indicator

## Visual Effects

## Usage Notes
