# Intimidating Shout

## Overview

Intimidating Shout unleashes a powerful roar that weakens nearby enemies and generates significant threat. This debuff ability is essential for tank builds, providing both crowd control and threat generation.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (8 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Stamina
**Debuff Duration:** 15 seconds

## Effects

**Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -20%
- Applies to all enemies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled by enemies
- Visual debuff effect

**Threat Generation:**
- Threat Increase: +100% for 10 seconds
- Applies to all enemies hit
- Essential for tanking
- High threat generation

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual shout effect
- Debuff visible on enemies
- Area effect coverage
- Tank utility ability

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Tank threat generation
- Weakening enemy groups
- Crowd control
- Group debuffing

**Combat Tips:**
- Use to generate threat on groups

- Essential for tank builds
