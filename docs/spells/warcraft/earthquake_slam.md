# Earthquake Slam

## Overview

Earthquake Slam slams the ground with immense force, dealing area damage and stunning all enemies in the impact radius. This rare ability provides both crowd control and area damage, making it essential for group combat.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (8 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 200% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Area coverage

**Crowd Control:**
- Stun Duration: 2.5 seconds
- Applies to all enemies in radius
- Cannot be resisted (guaranteed)
- Visual stun effect

**Additional Effects:**

- Effective crowd control
