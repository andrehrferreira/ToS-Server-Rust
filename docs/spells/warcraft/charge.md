# Charge

## Overview

Charge allows the warrior to quickly close the distance to an enemy, delivering a powerful strike and potentially stunning the target. This gap-closing ability is essential for melee combatants to engage ranged enemies.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 100% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Movement:**
- Dashes to target location
- Travels in straight line
- Cannot be interrupted during dash

**Crowd Control:**
- Stun Duration: 1.5 seconds (if target is moving)
- Stun Chance: 70% (if target is moving)
- No stun if target is stationary

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Warrior leans forward
- Weapon raised
- Dash preparation

**Movement:**
- Fast dash animation
- Trail effect behind warrior
- Dust kicked up

**Impact:**
- Weapon strike impact
- Stun visual effect (if applied)
- Knockback effect on target

**Sound Effects:**
- Dash whoosh sound
- Impact sound
- Stun sound (if applied)

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Closing distance to enemies
- Initiating combat
- Interrupting enemy actions
- Escaping dangerous situations (charge to ally)

**Combat Tips:**
- Use to engage ranged enemies
- Charge moving targets for stun
- Can charge through enemies (piercing)
- Use defensively to escape

**Positioning:**
- Charge from maximum range
- Target enemy backline
- Use terrain to your advantage
- Consider enemy positioning

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base damage (100% Attack Power), 1.5s stun
**Level 2:** +10% damage, +0.2s stun duration
**Level 3:** +20% damage, +0.4s stun duration
**Level 4:** +30% damage, +0.6s stun duration
**Level 5:** +40% damage, +0.8s stun duration, +5% stun chance
