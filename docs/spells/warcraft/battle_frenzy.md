# Battle Frenzy

## Overview

Battle Frenzy sends the warrior into a berserker rage, significantly increasing damage dealt and attack speed. This self-buff ability enhances all combat effectiveness, making it essential for melee DPS builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Stamina
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Damage Increase: +25%
- Attack Speed Increase: +30%
- Applies to all attacks and abilities
- Cannot be dispelled

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual frenzy effect
- Enhanced combat animations
- Stacks with equipment bonuses
- High DPS increase

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**

**Combat Tips:**

- Maintain buff during combat
