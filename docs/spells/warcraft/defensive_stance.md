# Defensive Stance

## Overview

Defensive Stance adopts a protective fighting posture that increases defense and reduces incoming damage. This defensive buff is essential for tank builds and survivability in difficult encounters.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds (toggle)
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina (activation)
**Duration:** Toggle (until deactivated)

## Effects

**Defense:**
- Damage Reduction: +30%
- Physical Resistance: +25%
- Applies to all damage types
- Stacks with other defensive abilities

**Movement:**
- Movement Speed: -20%
- Cannot sprint while active
- Can still attack and use abilities

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual defensive stance
- Can be toggled on/off
- Essential for tanking
- High survivability increase

## Visual Effects

**Active State:**

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Tank survivability
- Reducing incoming damage
- Defensive builds
- Difficult encounters

**Combat Tips:**
- Activate before taking damage
- Use with Shield Wall for maximum defense
- Toggle off when mobility needed
- Essential for tank builds

**Defensive Synergy:**
- Combines with Shield Wall
- Works with Iron Skin
- Stacks with Guardian's Aegis
- Maximum defense potential

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base (+30% reduction, +25% resistance), -20% movement
**Level 2:** +5% reduction, +5% resistance, -15% movement penalty
**Level 3:** +10% reduction, +10% resistance, -10% movement penalty
**Level 4:** +15% reduction, +15% resistance, -5% movement penalty
**Level 5:** +20% reduction, +20% resistance, no movement penalty
