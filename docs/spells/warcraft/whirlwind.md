# Whirlwind

## Overview

Whirlwind spins the warrior in a circle, dealing damage to all nearby enemies. This area-of-effect ability is essential for dealing with multiple enemies and provides excellent crowd control through damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (5 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 90% of Attack Power per spin
- Number of Spins: 4 spins over duration
- Total Damage: 360% of Attack Power (per enemy)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per spin)

**Area Effect:**
- Hits all enemies in radius
- Continuous damage while channeling
- Movement speed reduced by 50% while channeling
- Can be interrupted by damage

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual spin effect
- Area coverage
- Multiple hits per enemy
- Effective crowd control

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Spin initiation

**Active State:**
- Rapid spinning animation

**Sound Effects:**
- Spin initiation sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Area damage on multiple enemies
- Clearing groups of enemies
- Crowd control through damage
- Melee area attacks

**Combat Tips:**
- Use when surrounded by enemies
- Position in center of groups
- Use after Charge for engagement
- Combine with defensive abilities

**Area Synergy:**
- Combines with Earthquake Slam
- Works with other area abilities
- Effective for group combat
- Essential for tank builds
