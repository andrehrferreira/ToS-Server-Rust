# Power Strike

## Overview

Power Strike channels all strength into a single, devastating blow that deals massive damage to the target. This high-damage ability is perfect for finishing off enemies or dealing burst damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 250% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (200% critical damage)
- Ignores 30% of target's armor

**Additional Effects:**
- High single-target damage
- Visual power effect
- Can interrupt enemy actions
- Effective finisher

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**

**Impact:**

**Sound Effects:**

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- High single-target damage
- Finishing off enemies
- Burst damage
- Interrupting enemies

**Combat Tips:**
- Use after Charge for combo
- Combine with Battle Frenzy for damage
- Use for finishing blows
- Interrupt enemy casts

**Damage Synergy:**
- Combines with Charge
- Works with Battle Frenzy
- Enhanced by Berserker Rage
- High burst damage potential
