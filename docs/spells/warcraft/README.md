# Warcraft Skillset

## Overview

Warcraft focuses on powerful melee combat abilities, devastating close-range attacks, and abilities that strengthen the warrior while weakening enemies. This skillset is ideal for players who prefer aggressive, in-your-face combat styles.

## Skillset Theme

**Combat Style:** Melee DPS/Tank
**Primary Role:** Damage Dealer / Tank
**Secondary Role:** Debuffer
**Resource:** Stamina/Mana hybrid

## Spell List

### Common Spells (7)

1. **[Charge](./charge.md)** - Gap closer and stun
2. **[Triple Slash](./triple_slash.md)** - Three-hit combo attack
3. **[Battle Frenzy](./battle_frenzy.md)** - Damage and attack speed buff

5. **[Intimidating Shout](./intimidating_shout.md)** - Debuff and threat generation
6. **[Power Strike](./power_strike.md)** - High damage single hit
7. **[Defensive Stance](./defensive_stance.md)** - Defense buff

### Rare Spells (4)

## Synergies

**Good Combinations:**
- **Fortitude**: Defense and tanking capabilities
- **Shadowcraft**: Mobility and burst damage
- **Auraweaving**: Buffs and mobility

**Playstyle:**
- Aggressive melee combat
- Close-range engagement
- High damage output
- Can tank or DPS depending on build
