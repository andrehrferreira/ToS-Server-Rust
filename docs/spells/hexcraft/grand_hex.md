# Grand Hex

## Overview

Grand Hex is the ultimate hexcraft ability, applying a comprehensive hex that deals massive damage, applies multiple debuffs, and provides extended control. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of hex mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 25 units
**Cast Time:** 3.5 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 180 Mana
**Duration:** 40 seconds

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Mental Damage: 20% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 800% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Mental/Magic
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per tick)

**Debuffs:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -50%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -60%
- Accuracy Reduction: -50%
- Ability Lock: 75% chance
- All debuffs apply simultaneously

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Cannot be interrupted once started
- Movement speed reduced by 90% while channeling

**Additional Effects:**
- Ultimate comprehensive hex
- Maximum hex potential
- Essential ultimate ability
- Ultimate control and damage
