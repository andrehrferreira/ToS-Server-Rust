# Hex Mastery

## Overview

Hex Mastery enhances all hex abilities, increasing their effectiveness and duration. This rare buff ability maximizes the power of all hex magic spells.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 65 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Hex Effectiveness: +50%
- Hex Duration: +50%
- Applies to all hex abilities
- Cannot be dispelled
