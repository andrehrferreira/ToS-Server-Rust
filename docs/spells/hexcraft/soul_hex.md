# Soul Hex

## Overview

Soul Hex applies a powerful hex that deals continuous mental damage and applies multiple debuffs simultaneously. This rare ability provides both damage and comprehensive debuffing.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 75 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Mental Damage: 15% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 450% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Mental/Magic
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per tick)

**Debuffs:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -30%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -35%
- Accuracy Reduction: -25%
- All debuffs apply simultaneously

**Additional Effects:**
- Powerful single-target hex
- Comprehensive debuffing
- Essential hex ability
- High utility ability
