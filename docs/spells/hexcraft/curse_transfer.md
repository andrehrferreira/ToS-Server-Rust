# Curse Transfer

## Overview

Curse Transfer moves a harmful debuff from the caster or ally to an enemy target. This utility hex provides debuff removal and enemy debuffing simultaneously.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self/Ally to Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana

## Effects

**Transfer:**
- Moves one debuff from caster/ally to enemy
- Debuff retains original duration
- Cannot transfer certain boss debuffs
- Visual transfer effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Debuff removal and application
- Essential utility ability
- High utility ability
- Core utility ability
