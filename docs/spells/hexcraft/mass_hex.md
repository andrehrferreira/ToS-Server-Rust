# Mass Hex

## Overview

Mass Hex applies multiple hexes to all enemies in an area simultaneously, providing comprehensive area debuffing. This rare ability provides exceptional area control.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 2.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 85 Mana

## Effects

**Hexes:**
- Weakness Hex: -25% damage dealt
- Confusion Hex: -30% accuracy
- Hex Mark: +30% hex damage taken
- All hexes apply simultaneously
- Duration: 20 seconds

**Additional Effects:**
- Area hex application
- Comprehensive debuffing
- Essential area ability
