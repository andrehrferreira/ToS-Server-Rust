# Hex Shield

## Overview

Hex Shield creates a protective barrier of hex energy that increases magic resistance and reduces mental damage taken. This defensive hex provides survivability against magical attacks.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds
