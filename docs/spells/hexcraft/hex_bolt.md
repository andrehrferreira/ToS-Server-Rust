# Hex Bolt

## Overview

Hex Bolt launches a bolt of hex energy that deals mental damage and applies a hex effect to the target. This fundamental hex magic spell provides reliable damage with mental manipulation properties.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Projectile
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 16 units
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 4 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 22 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Mental Damage: 125% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Mental/Magic
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Hex Effect:**
- Hex Chance: 30%
- Hex Duration: 8 seconds
- Hex Effect: -15% accuracy, -10% movement speed

**Additional Effects:**
- Mental energy trail
- Visual hex mark on target
- Can stack with other hexes

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Single target mental damage
- Applying hex effects
- Hex magic builds
- Control setup

**Combat Tips:**
- Use to apply hexes
- Combine with other hex spells
- Stack hexes for maximum effect

**Hex Synergy:**
- Combines with other hex spells
- Stacks with Hex Mastery
