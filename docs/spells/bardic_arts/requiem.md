# Requiem

## Overview

Requiem plays a devastating melody that deals area damage and applies powerful debuffs to all enemies. This rare ability provides both damage and crowd control.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 2.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 90 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Magic Damage: 200% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Magic/Sonic
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Hits all enemies in radius

**Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -30%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -40%
- Duration: 20 seconds
- Applies to all enemies

**Additional Effects:**
