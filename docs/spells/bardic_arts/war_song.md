# War Song

## Overview

War Song is an inspiring melody that increases the attack power of all nearby allies. This group buff ability enhances the combat effectiveness of the entire party, making it essential for group content.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (15 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Attack Power Increase: +15%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Stacks with other buffs
- Visual musical effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Group-wide application
- Cannot be dispelled by enemies
- Refreshes duration if recast
- Musical notes visual effect

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Instrument playing animation
- Musical notes appearing
- Aura expansion

**Active State:**
- Musical notes around allies
- Buff visual effect
- Continuous music

**Sound Effects:**
- War song music
- Inspiring melody
- Continuous background music

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Group combat enhancement
- Raid and dungeon content
- Party buffs
- Long-duration buffs

**Combat Tips:**
- Use before engaging enemies
- Maintain buff throughout combat
- Combine with other group buffs
