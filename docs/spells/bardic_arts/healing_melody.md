# Healing Melody

## Overview

Healing Melody plays a soothing tune that provides continuous healing over time to all nearby allies. This healing-over-time ability is essential for sustained party support.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (3.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana
**Duration:** 15 seconds (healing continues after channel)

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 12% of Magic Power per second
- Total Healing: 180% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Healing Type: Over Time
- Applies to all allies in radius

**Additional Effects:**
- Continuous healing
- Group-wide support
- Essential healing ability
- Core support ability
