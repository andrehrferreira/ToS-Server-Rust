# Lullaby

## Overview

Lullaby plays a soothing melody that puts enemies to sleep, providing crowd control through sleep effects. This crowd control ability is essential for controlling enemy groups.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (8 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Mana
**Duration:** 10 seconds

## Effects

**Sleep:**
- Puts enemies to sleep
- Enemies cannot act while asleep
- Sleep breaks on taking damage
- Visual sleep effect

**Additional Effects:**
