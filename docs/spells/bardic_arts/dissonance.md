# Dissonance

## Overview

Dissonance plays a jarring melody that debuffs all nearby enemies, reducing their combat effectiveness. This debuff ability provides crowd control through enemy weakening.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (10 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 25 seconds

## Effects

**Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -20%
- Attack Speed Reduction: -25%
- Applies to all enemies in radius
- Visual dissonance effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Area debuff application
- Essential crowd control
- High utility ability
- Support debuff
