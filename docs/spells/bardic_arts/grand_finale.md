# Grand Finale

## Overview

Grand Finale is the ultimate bardic ability, playing a legendary finale that provides massive group buffs, area damage, and enemy debuffs simultaneously. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of musical mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies and Enemies
**Range:** Self (20 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 4.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 200 Mana

## Effects

**Ally Buffs:**
- Attack Power Increase: +50%
- Magic Power Increase: +50%
- Defense Increase: +40%
- Movement Speed Increase: +40%
- Duration: 60 seconds

**Enemy Damage:**
- Magic Damage: 300% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Magic/Sonic
- Hits all enemies in radius

**Enemy Debuffs:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -50%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -60%
- Duration: 30 seconds

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 95% while channeling
- Ultimate group support ability
- Maximum utility potential
