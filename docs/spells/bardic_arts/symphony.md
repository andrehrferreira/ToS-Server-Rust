# Symphony

Symphony plays multiple melodies simultaneously, providing various buffs and debuffs at once. This rare ability provides comprehensive group support and enemy control.

**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies and Enemies
**Range:** Self (15 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 50 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 110 Mana
