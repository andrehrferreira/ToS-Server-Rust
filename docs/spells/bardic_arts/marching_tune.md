# Marching Tune

## Overview

Marching Tune plays an inspiring march that increases movement speed for all nearby allies. This mobility buff is essential for group positioning and movement.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana
**Duration:** 40 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Movement Speed Increase: +30%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled
- Visual march effect

**Additional Effects:**
