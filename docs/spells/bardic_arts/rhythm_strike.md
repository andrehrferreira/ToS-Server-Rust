# Rhythm Strike

## Overview

Rhythm Strike channels musical energy into a powerful strike that deals damage based on the rhythm of the melody. This offensive ability provides damage output for bardic builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Magic Damage: 160% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Magic/Sonic
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Enhanced by other melodies

**Additional Effects:**
