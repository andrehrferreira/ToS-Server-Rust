# Bardic Mastery

## Overview

Bardic Mastery enhances all bardic abilities, increasing their effectiveness and duration. This rare buff ability maximizes the power of all musical spells.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 70 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Melody Effectiveness: +50%
- Melody Duration: +50%
- Applies to all bardic abilities
- Cannot be dispelled

**Additional Effects:**
