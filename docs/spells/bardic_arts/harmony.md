# Harmony

## Overview

Harmony plays a balanced melody that increases all stats for all nearby allies. This comprehensive group buff is essential for party enhancement.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 45 Mana
**Duration:** 50 seconds
