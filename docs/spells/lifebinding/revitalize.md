# Revitalize

## Overview

Revitalize restores both mana and stamina to a target, providing resource regeneration. This utility ability is essential for maintaining party resources and combat effectiveness.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Mana

## Effects

**Resource Restoration:**
- Mana Restored: 25% of maximum mana
- Stamina Restored: 30% of maximum stamina
- Instant restoration
- Cannot overheal resources

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual revitalization effect
- Resource restoration visual
- Essential utility ability
- High resource support
