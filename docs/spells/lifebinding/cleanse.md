# Cleanse

## Overview

Cleanse removes harmful debuffs and conditions from a target, restoring them to full combat effectiveness. This utility ability is essential for countering enemy debuffs and maintaining party effectiveness.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana

## Effects

**Cleansing:**
- Removes all debuffs
- Removes all conditions (Burn, Freeze, Poison, etc.)
- Cannot remove certain boss debuffs
- Instant effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual cleansing effect
- Debuff removal visual
- Restores full effectiveness
- Essential utility ability

## Visual Effects

## Usage Notes

- Removing debuffs

**Combat Tips:**

- Remove dangerous conditions quickly

**Utility Synergy:**
- Combines with healing abilities

- Essential for group content

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base (removes all debuffs and conditions)
**Level 2:** Can remove 1 additional debuff type
**Level 3:** -1s cooldown
**Level 4:** Can remove 2 additional debuff types
**Level 5:** -2s cooldown, removes boss debuffs (some)
