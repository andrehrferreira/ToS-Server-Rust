# Mass Heal

## Overview

Mass Heal channels massive life energy to restore health to all allies in a large area simultaneously. This rare area healing ability provides exceptional group healing, essential for raid content.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (15 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 100 Mana

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 250% of Magic Power
- Healing Type: Direct (instant)
- Can Critical Heal: Yes
- Applies to all allies in large radius

**Additional Effects:**
- Large area coverage
- Visual mass healing effect
- Cannot overheal (max health limit)
- Essential for raid content
