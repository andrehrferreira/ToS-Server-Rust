# Lifebinding Skillset

## Overview

Lifebinding focuses on healing and support abilities, allowing players to restore health, remove debuffs, and provide protective buffs to themselves and allies. This skillset is essential for support roles and solo survivability.

## Skillset Theme

**Combat Style:** Support/Healer
**Primary Role:** Healer / Support
**Secondary Role:** Buff Provider
**Resource:** Mana

## Spell List

### Common Spells (7)

1. **[Heal](./heal.md)** - Single target healing
2. **[Regeneration](./regeneration.md)** - Healing over time
3. **[Group Heal](./group_heal.md)** - Area healing
4. **[Cleanse](./cleanse.md)** - Remove debuffs
5. **[Shield of Light](./shield_of_light.md)** - Damage absorption shield
6. **[Revitalize](./revitalize.md)** - Mana and stamina restore
7. **[Blessing](./blessing.md)** - Stat buff

### Rare Spells (4)

8. **[Mass Heal](./mass_heal.md)** - Large area healing
9. **[Resurrection](./resurrection.md)** - Revive fallen ally
10. **[Divine Protection](./divine_protection.md)** - Powerful damage reduction
11. **[Life Surge](./life_surge.md)** - Massive instant heal

### Ancient Spell (1)

12. **[Divine Intervention](./divine_intervention.md)** - Ultimate support ability

## Synergies

**Good Combinations:**
- **Fortitude**: Tank and healer combination
- **Auraweaving**: Additional buffs and mobility
- **Elementalism**: Self-healing for mage builds

**Playstyle:**
- Support allies in combat
- Maintain party health
- Remove harmful effects
- Provide buffs and protection
