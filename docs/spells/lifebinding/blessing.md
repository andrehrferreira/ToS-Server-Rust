# Blessing

## Overview

Blessing bestows a divine blessing upon a target, increasing their combat effectiveness through enhanced stats. This buff ability provides party support and combat enhancement.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 25 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Attack Power Increase: +15%
- Magic Power Increase: +15%
- Defense Increase: +10%
- Applies to all stats

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual blessing effect
- Cannot be dispelled
- Stacks with equipment bonuses
- High combat enhancement

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Blessing preparation
- Divine energy gathering
- Blessing application

**Active State:**
- Blessing aura on target
- Divine particles
- Enhanced visual effect

**Sound Effects:**
- Blessing sound
- Divine chime
- Enhancement sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Stat enhancement
- Party buffing
- Combat effectiveness
- Support builds

**Combat Tips:**
- Use before engaging enemies
- Apply to all party members
- Maintain buffs during combat
- Essential for support builds

**Buff Synergy:**
- Combines with other buffs
- Works with healing abilities
- Essential for group content
- High support utility

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base (+15% attack, +15% magic, +10% defense), 60s duration
**Level 2:** +2% all stats, +10s duration
**Level 3:** +4% all stats, +20s duration
**Level 4:** +6% all stats, +30s duration
**Level 5:** +8% all stats, +40s duration, -3s cooldown
