# Heal

## Overview

Heal channels life energy into a target, restoring health instantly. This fundamental healing spell provides reliable single-target healing for both self and allies.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 4 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 200% of Magic Power
- Healing Type: Direct (instant)
- Can Critical Heal: Yes (150% healing)

**Additional Effects:**
- Instant health restoration
- Visual healing effect on target
- Cannot overheal (max health limit)

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Light gathering in hands
- Channeling animation
- Release animation

**Effect:**
- Golden light on target
- Healing particles
- Health bar update

**Sound Effects:**
- Light gathering sound
- Healing chime
- Completion sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Single target healing
- Emergency healing
- Maintaining ally health
- Self-healing in combat

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on low-health allies
- Heal yourself when taking damage
- Combine with Regeneration for sustained healing
- Use before entering dangerous situations

**Healing Synergy:**
- Combines with Regeneration for over-time healing
- Group Heal for multiple targets
- Mass Heal for large groups

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base healing (200% Magic Power)
**Level 2:** +10% healing (220% Magic Power)
**Level 3:** +20% healing (240% Magic Power)
**Level 4:** +30% healing (260% Magic Power)
**Level 5:** +40% healing (280% Magic Power), -0.5s cast time
