# Group Heal

## Overview

Group Heal channels life energy to restore health to all nearby allies simultaneously. This area healing ability is essential for group content and healing multiple allies at once.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (10 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 180% of Magic Power
- Healing Type: Direct (instant)
- Can Critical Heal: Yes
- Applies to all allies in radius

**Additional Effects:**
- Group-wide healing
- Visual healing effect
- Cannot overheal (max health limit)
- Essential for group content

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Life energy gathering
- Group healing channeling
- Release animation

**Effect:**
- Healing wave effect
- Healing particles on all allies
- Group healing visual

**Sound Effects:**
- Group healing sound
- Healing wave sound
- Multiple healing chimes

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Group healing
- Raid and dungeon content
- Healing multiple allies
- Party support

**Combat Tips:**
- Use when multiple allies need healing
- Position for maximum coverage
- Use in group content
- Coordinate with team

**Group Synergy:**

- Works with other group buffs
- Essential for group content
