# Divine Protection

## Overview

Divine Protection creates an impenetrable barrier of divine energy that provides massive damage reduction. This rare defensive ability provides exceptional survivability, essential for protecting priority targets.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 80 Mana
**Duration:** 15 seconds

## Effects

- Damage Reduction: +60%

- Visual divine barrier
