# Shield of Light

## Overview

Shield of Light creates a protective barrier of light energy that absorbs incoming damage. This defensive ability provides survivability for allies, essential for protecting party members.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Shield:**
- Damage Absorption: 300% of Magic Power
- Absorbs all damage types
- Shield breaks when absorption depleted
- Visual shield effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual shield on target
- Cannot be dispelled
- High survivability increase
- Essential protection ability

## Usage Notes
