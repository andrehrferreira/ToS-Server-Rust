# Regeneration

## Overview

Regeneration channels life energy into a target, providing continuous healing over time. This healing-over-time ability is essential for sustained healing and mana efficiency.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 15% of Magic Power per second
- Total Healing: 300% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Healing Type: Over Time
- Can Critical Heal: Yes (per tick)

**Additional Effects:**
- Continuous healing ticks
- Visual regeneration effect
- Cannot overheal (max health limit)
- Efficient mana usage

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Life energy gathering
- Regeneration channeling
- Release animation

**Effect:**
- Continuous healing particles
- Regeneration aura on target
- Health bar updates

**Sound Effects:**
- Regeneration sound
- Continuous healing chime
- Completion sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Sustained healing
- Mana-efficient healing
- Pre-combat preparation
- Long-duration healing

**Combat Tips:**
- Use before combat starts
- Combine with Heal for burst
- Use for sustained healing

**Healing Synergy:**
- Combines with Heal
