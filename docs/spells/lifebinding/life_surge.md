# Life Surge

## Overview

Life Surge channels immense life energy into a target, providing a massive instant heal. This rare healing ability provides exceptional burst healing, essential for emergency situations.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 25 units
**Cast Time:** 2.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 70 Mana

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 400% of Magic Power
- Healing Type: Direct (instant)
- Can Critical Heal: Yes (200% healing)
- Massive burst healing

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual life surge effect
- Cannot overheal (max health limit)
- Essential for emergency healing
- High burst healing potential
