# Resurrection

## Overview

Resurrection brings a fallen ally back to life, restoring them to combat with partial health and mana. This rare revival ability is essential for group content and preventing party wipes.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Dead Ally
**Range:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** 5.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 150 Mana

## Effects

**Revival:**
- Restores target to life
- Health Restored: 50% of maximum health
- Mana Restored: 50% of maximum mana
- Removes death debuff

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 80% while channeling
- Visual resurrection effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual resurrection effect
- Cannot be used in combat (some restrictions)
- Essential for group content
- Prevents party wipes
