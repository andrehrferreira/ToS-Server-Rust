# Divine Intervention

## Overview

Divine Intervention is the ultimate lifebinding ability, channeling the full power of divine energy to provide massive healing, protection, and resource restoration to all nearby allies. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of healing mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (20 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 4.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 200 Mana

## Effects

**Healing:**
- Health Restored: 500% of Magic Power
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Can Critical Heal: Yes

**Protection:**
- Damage Reduction: +40% for 30 seconds
- Applies to all allies
- Cannot be dispelled

**Resource Restoration:**
- Mana Restored: 40% of maximum mana
- Stamina Restored: 50% of maximum stamina
- Applies to all allies

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 90% while channeling
- Ultimate support ability
- Maximum group support
