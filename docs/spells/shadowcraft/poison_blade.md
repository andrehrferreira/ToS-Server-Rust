# Poison Blade

## Overview

Poison Blade coats the weapon with deadly poison, dealing damage over time with each strike. This damage-over-time ability provides sustained damage and condition application.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Poison:**
- Poison Damage: 8% of Attack Power per second
- Applies to all weapon attacks
- Duration: 30 seconds
- Stacks with other poisons

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual poison effect on weapon
- Poison application on hit
- Damage over time
- Essential for DoT builds

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Damage over time
- Sustained damage
- Poison builds

**Combat Tips:**

- Combine with other attacks
