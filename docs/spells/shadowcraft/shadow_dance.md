# Shadow Dance

## Overview

Shadow Dance unleashes a flurry of shadow-enhanced strikes, dealing multiple rapid attacks to the target. This rare ability provides high burst damage through multiple hits.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.5 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 35 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 55 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Strikes: 8 strikes
- Damage per Strike: 100% of Attack Power
- Total Damage: 800% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per strike)

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 60% while channeling
- Each strike every 0.3 seconds

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual shadow dance effect
- Rapid strikes animation
- High burst damage potential
- Essential burst ability
