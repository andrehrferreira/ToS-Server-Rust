# Shadow Strike

## Overview

Shadow Strike delivers a quick, precise strike enhanced by shadow energy, dealing high damage with increased critical strike chance. This rapid attack ability provides consistent damage output.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 140% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Critical Strike Chance: +30%
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Additional Effects:**
- Quick attack speed
- Visual shadow effect
- High critical potential
- Consistent damage output
