# Shadowcraft Skillset

## Overview

Shadowcraft focuses on stealth, quick attacks, and evasion. This skillset provides abilities for stealth gameplay, backstab attacks, hit-and-run tactics, and high critical strike potential. Perfect for players who prefer agile, sneaky combat styles.

## Skillset Theme

**Combat Style:** Stealth/Assassin
**Primary Role:** Burst Damage Dealer
**Secondary Role:** Crowd Control
**Resource:** Stamina/Mana hybrid

2. **[Backstab](./backstab.md)** - High damage back attack

5. **[Evade](./evade.md)** - Dodge and counter
6. **[Shadow Strike](./shadow_strike.md)** - Quick melee attack

## Synergies

**Good Combinations:**
- **Marksmanship**: Stealth and ranged attacks
- **Warcraft**: Burst damage combination
- **Auraweaving**: Mobility and buffs

**Playstyle:**
- Stealth and positioning
- High burst damage from stealth
- Hit-and-run tactics
- Critical strike focus
