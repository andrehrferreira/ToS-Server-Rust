# Evade

## Overview

Evade allows the caster to quickly dodge an incoming attack and counter-attack. This defensive ability provides both survivability and counter-attack potential.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina
**Duration:** 1.5 seconds (dodge window)

## Effects

**Dodge:**
- Evasion: 100% (during window)
- Brief invulnerability
- Can dodge any attack
- Visual dodge effect

**Counter-Attack:**
- Counter Damage: 150% of Attack Power
- Triggers automatically on successful dodge
- Instant counter strike
- High damage potential

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual dodge animation
- Counter-attack visual
- High skill requirement
- Essential defensive ability

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base (100% evasion, 150% counter, 1.5s window)
**Level 2:** +0.2s window, +25% counter damage
**Level 3:** +0.4s window, +50% counter damage
