# Assassinate

## Overview

Assassinate channels all shadow energy into a single, devastating strike that deals massive damage, especially from stealth or behind the target. This rare ability provides the highest single-target damage in shadowcraft.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 500% of Attack Power (from front)
- Physical Damage: 1500% of Attack Power (from behind or stealth)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (400% critical from behind)

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 70% while channeling
- Visual assassination preparation

**Additional Effects:**
- Can execute enemies below 25% health (from behind)
- Visual assassination effect
- Ultimate single-target damage
- Essential assassination ability

- Ultimate single-target damage
