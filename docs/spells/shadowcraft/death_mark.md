# Death Mark

## Overview

Death Mark is the ultimate shadowcraft ability, marking a target for death and dealing catastrophic damage when the mark is triggered. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of assassination mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 25 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Mark Duration:** 10 seconds

## Effects

**Mark:**
- Places death mark on target
- Mark visible to caster only
- Target takes +50% damage from caster
- Mark cannot be dispelled

**Trigger Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 1000% of Attack Power (if triggered by attack)
- Physical Damage: 2000% of Attack Power (if triggered from behind or stealth)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (500% critical)

**Trigger Conditions:**
- Mark triggers on next attack from caster
- Enhanced damage if from behind or stealth
- Can execute enemies below 30% health
- Visual mark effect

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Ultimate single-target damage
- Boss encounters
- Executing priority targets
- Maximum assassination damage

**Combat Tips:**
- Apply mark before engaging
- Trigger from behind for maximum damage
- Use for execute on low-health enemies
- Coordinate with team for maximum effect

**Ultimate Synergy:**
- Combines with Stealth
- Works with Shadow Step
- Enhanced by all shadowcraft abilities
- Maximum assassination potential
