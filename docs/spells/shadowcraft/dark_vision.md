# Dark Vision

## Overview

Dark Vision enhances the caster's perception, increasing critical strike chance and detection range. This buff ability improves combat effectiveness and awareness.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Critical Strike Chance: +35%
- Detection Range: +50%
- Can see stealthed enemies (within range)
- Applies to all attacks

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual dark vision effect
- Enhanced awareness
- Cannot be dispelled
- Essential for critical builds

## Visual Effects

**Activation:**
- Dark vision activation
- Perception enhancement
- Buff activation

**Active State:**
- Dark vision aura
- Enhanced visual effects
- Perception particles

**Sound Effects:**
- Dark vision sound
- Perception enhancement sound
- Awareness sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Critical strike builds
- Enhanced awareness
- Detection of stealth
- Combat effectiveness

**Combat Tips:**
- Use before engaging enemies
- Maintain buff during combat
- Use for critical builds
- Essential for assassination builds

**Critical Synergy:**
- Combines with all attacks
- Works with Backstab
- Enhanced by critical builds
- Maximum critical potential
