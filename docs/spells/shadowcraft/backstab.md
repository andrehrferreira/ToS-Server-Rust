# Backstab

## Overview

Backstab delivers a devastating strike from behind the target, dealing massive damage when attacking from stealth or behind the enemy. This fundamental assassination ability is essential for shadowcraft builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 200% of Attack Power (from front)
- Physical Damage: 600% of Attack Power (from behind or stealth)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (300% critical from behind)

**Position Requirement:**
- Must be behind target or in stealth
- 3x damage multiplier from behind
- Visual backstab indicator

**Additional Effects:**
- High burst damage potential
- Essential for stealth builds
- Critical strike focus

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Backstab preparation
- Strike animation
- Impact effect

**Impact:**
- Massive strike effect
- Critical visual (if from behind)
- Backstab indicator

**Sound Effects:**
- Backstab sound
- Strike impact sound
- Critical sound (if from behind)

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- High burst damage
- Stealth attacks
- Assassination builds
- Finishing enemies

**Combat Tips:**
- Use from stealth for maximum damage
- Position behind target
- Combine with Shadow Step
- Essential for assassination builds

**Stealth Synergy:**
- Combines with Stealth
- Works with Shadow Step
- Enhanced by Dark Vision
- Maximum burst damage potential
