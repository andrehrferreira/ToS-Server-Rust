# Mass Confusion

## Overview

Mass Confusion unleashes a wave of mental energy that confuses all enemies in an area, causing them to attack randomly. This rare crowd control ability provides exceptional area control.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (10 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 2.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 80 Mana
**Duration:** 8 seconds
