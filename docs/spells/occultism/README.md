# Occultism Skillset

## Overview

Occultism focuses on mind-affecting magic, debuffs, and crowd control. This skillset provides abilities to weaken enemies, control their actions, and apply various negative effects. Perfect for players who prefer strategic, control-oriented combat.

## Skillset Theme

**Combat Style:** Control/Debuff
**Primary Role:** Crowd Control
**Secondary Role:** Debuffer
**Resource:** Mana

## Spell List

### Common Spells (7)

1. **[Mind Blast](./mind_blast.md)** - Mental damage and stun
2. **[Curse of Weakness](./curse_of_weakness.md)** - Damage reduction debuff

4. **[Charm](./charm.md)** - Temporary enemy control

6. **[Mental Barrier](./mental_barrier.md)** - Magic resistance buff
7. **[Soul Drain](./soul_drain.md)** - Mana drain and damage

## Synergies

**Good Combinations:**
- **Elementalism**: Control and damage combination
- **Hexcraft**: Enhanced mental manipulation
- **Auraweaving**: Additional debuffs

**Playstyle:**
- Control enemy actions
- Apply debuffs for team advantage
- Disable priority targets
- Strategic combat focus
