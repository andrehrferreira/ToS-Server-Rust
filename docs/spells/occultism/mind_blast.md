# Mind Blast

## Overview

Mind Blast unleashes a burst of mental energy that deals mental damage and potentially stuns the target. This fundamental mental attack ability provides both damage and crowd control.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** 1.2 seconds
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 28 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Mental Damage: 140% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Mental/Magic
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Conditions:**
- Stun Chance: 30%
- Stun Duration: 2.0 seconds
- Interrupts enemy actions

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual mental blast
- Crowd control potential
- Essential mental attack
- Core occultism ability
