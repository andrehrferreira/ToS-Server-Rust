# Mind Control

## Overview

Mind Control is the ultimate occultism ability, taking complete and absolute control of an enemy for an extended duration. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of mental manipulation mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 25 units
**Cast Time:** 4.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 150 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds
