# Fear

## Overview

Fear causes the target to flee in terror, losing control of their character temporarily. This crowd control ability is essential for controlling enemy positioning and interrupting their actions.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana
**Duration:** 4 seconds

## Effects

**Crowd Control:**
- Fear Duration: 4 seconds
- Target flees from caster
- Cannot attack or use abilities
- Moves away randomly

**Additional Effects:**
- Interrupts current actions
- Cannot be controlled by player
- Breaks on taking damage (50% chance)
- Visual fear effect

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Dark energy gathering
- Mental projection animation
- Release animation

**Effect:**
- Fear aura on target
- Panic animation
- Fleeing movement

**Sound Effects:**
- Dark energy sound
- Fear scream
- Panic sounds

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Crowd control
- Interrupting enemy actions
- Separating enemies
- Creating space

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on dangerous enemies
- Fear healers to interrupt healing
- Use to separate groups
- Combine with area damage

**Control Synergy:**
- Combines with other CC abilities
- Works with Charm for extended control
- Use with Mass Confusion for group control

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base duration (4 seconds)
**Level 2:** +0.5s duration
**Level 3:** +1s duration
**Level 4:** +1.5s duration
**Level 5:** +2s duration, -20% break chance on damage
