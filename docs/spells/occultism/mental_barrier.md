# Mental Barrier

## Overview

Mental Barrier creates a protective mental shield that increases magic resistance and reduces mental damage taken. This defensive ability provides survivability against magical attacks.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds
