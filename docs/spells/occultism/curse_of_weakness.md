# Curse of Weakness

## Overview

Curse of Weakness applies a debilitating curse that significantly reduces the target's damage output and effectiveness. This debuff ability provides crowd control through damage reduction.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana
**Duration:** 25 seconds

## Effects

**Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -30%
- Attack Power Reduction: -25%
- Cannot be dispelled by target
- Visual curse effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Reduces enemy effectiveness
- Essential for crowd control
- High utility ability
- Support debuff
