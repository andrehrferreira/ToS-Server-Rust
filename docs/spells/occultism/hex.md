# Hex

## Overview

Hex applies multiple debilitating effects to the target, reducing their combat effectiveness through various debuffs. This multi-debuff ability provides comprehensive enemy weakening.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.8 seconds
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Debuffs:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -20%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -30%
- Attack Speed Reduction: -25%
- All debuffs apply simultaneously

**Additional Effects:**
- Multiple debuff application
- Comprehensive weakening
- Essential debuff ability
- High utility ability
