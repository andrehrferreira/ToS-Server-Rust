# Dominate Mind

## Overview

Dominate Mind takes complete control of an enemy for an extended duration, forcing them to fight for the caster. This rare control ability provides exceptional single-target control.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 100 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Control:**
- Complete enemy control
- Enemy fights for caster
- Cannot attack caster or allies
- Extended duration control

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 70% while channeling

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual mind domination
- Extended control duration
- Essential control ability
- High utility ability
