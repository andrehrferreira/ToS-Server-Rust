# Charm

## Overview

Charm temporarily controls an enemy, forcing them to fight for the caster. This crowd control ability provides both control and additional damage through charmed enemies.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 45 Mana
**Duration:** 12 seconds

## Effects

**Control:**
- Enemy fights for caster
- Cannot attack caster or allies
- Attacks other enemies
- Visual charm effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Crowd control ability
- Additional damage source
- Essential control ability
- High utility ability
