# Soul Drain

## Overview

Soul Drain channels dark energy to drain both mana and health from the target, dealing damage while restoring resources. This life-steal ability provides both offense and resource management.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.5 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 100% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 250% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Dark/Mental
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Drain:**
- Mana Restored: 20% of damage dealt
- Health Restored: 15% of damage dealt
- Resource management ability

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual soul drain
- Life and mana steal
- Essential resource ability
- High utility ability
