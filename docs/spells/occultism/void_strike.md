# Void Strike

## Overview

Void Strike channels void energy into a devastating strike that deals massive dark damage and applies a powerful debuff. This rare ability provides both high damage and debuff application.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 70 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 350% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Dark/Mental
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Debuff:**
- Damage Taken Increase: +30%
- Duration: 15 seconds
- Cannot be dispelled by target
- Visual void mark

**Additional Effects:**
- High single-target damage
- Powerful debuff application
- Essential damage ability
- High utility ability
