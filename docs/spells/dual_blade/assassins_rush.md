# Assassin's Rush

## Overview

Assassin's Rush dashes to the target and delivers a devastating dual-blade strike, dealing massive burst damage. This rare mobility attack ability provides both positioning and high damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target (Movement)
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 450% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (300% critical damage)
- Ignores 50% of target's armor

**Movement:**
- Instant dash to target
- Strikes on arrival
- Brief invulnerability during dash (0.3 seconds)
- Visual rush effect

**Additional Effects:**
- High burst damage potential
- Mobility and damage combo
- Essential burst ability
- High utility ability
