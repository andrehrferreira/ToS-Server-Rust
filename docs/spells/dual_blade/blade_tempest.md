# Blade Tempest

## Overview

Blade Tempest is the ultimate dual blade ability, creating a catastrophic storm of blades that devastates all enemies in a massive area. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of dual-wielding mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (15 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (4.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 150 Stamina
**Duration:** 5.0 seconds (continuous tempest)

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 150% of Attack Power per second
- Total Damage: 750% of Attack Power (per enemy, over duration)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per tick)

**Area Effect:**
- Massive area coverage
- Continuous damage over duration
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 95% while channeling

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual blade tempest
- Catastrophic area effect
- Ultimate area damage
- Maximum devastation potential
