# Dual Strike

## Overview

Dual Strike allows the warrior to attack with both weapons simultaneously, dealing damage from both blades in a single strike. This fundamental dual-wield ability maximizes damage output through weapon synergy.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 15 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Main Hand Damage: 100% of Attack Power
- Off Hand Damage: 75% of Attack Power
- Total Damage: 175% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (each weapon separately)

**Additional Effects:**
- Requires dual-wield weapons
- Both weapons strike simultaneously
- Visual dual strike effect

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Both weapons raised
- Strike preparation
- Dual attack animation

**Impact:**
- Two simultaneous strikes
- Crossed blade effect
- Impact particles

**Sound Effects:**
- Dual weapon sounds
- Strike impact sounds
- Blade clash sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Maximum single target damage
- Dual-wield builds
- Burst damage combos
- Fast damage output

**Combat Tips:**
- Use as primary attack in dual-wield builds
- Combine with attack speed buffs
- Use in rapid attack combos
- Maximize dual-wield potential

**Dual-Wield Synergy:**
- Combines with Dual Wield Mastery
- Works with Blade Flurry for combos
- Enhanced by Dual Mastery

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base damage (175% Attack Power total)
**Level 2:** +5% off-hand damage (180% total)
**Level 3:** +10% off-hand damage (185% total)
**Level 4:** +15% off-hand damage (190% total)
**Level 5:** +20% off-hand damage (195% total), -1s cooldown
