# Blade Flurry

## Overview

Blade Flurry unleashes a rapid flurry of dual-wield strikes, dealing multiple hits in quick succession. This combo ability provides high damage output through rapid attacks.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (1.5 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Strikes: 6 strikes
- Damage per Strike: 85% of Attack Power
- Total Damage: 510% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per strike)

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 40% while channeling
- Each strike every 0.25 seconds

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual blade flurry
- Rapid strikes animation
- High burst damage potential
- Essential combo ability
