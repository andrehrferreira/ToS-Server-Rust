# Dual Wield Mastery

## Overview

Dual Wield Mastery enhances the warrior's dual-wielding capabilities, significantly increasing attack speed and damage. This self-buff ability maximizes the effectiveness of all dual-wield attacks.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Stamina
**Duration:** 30 seconds

- Damage Increase: +20%
