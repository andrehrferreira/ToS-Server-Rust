# Parry

## Overview

Parry allows the warrior to deflect incoming attacks and counter-attack immediately. This defensive attack ability provides both protection and offense.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self (Reactive)
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant (reactive)
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina
**Parry Window:** 1.0 seconds

## Effects

**Parry:**
- Damage Reduction: +90% (during window)
- Blocks all melee attacks
- Requires dual-wield weapons
- Visual parry effect

**Counter:**
- Counter Damage: 200% of Attack Power
- Triggers automatically on successful parry
- Cannot be interrupted
- High damage potential

**Additional Effects:**
- High skill requirement
- Brief protection window
- Essential defensive ability
- Core defensive skill
