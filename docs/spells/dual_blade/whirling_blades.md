# Whirling Blades

## Overview

Whirling Blades spins the warrior with both blades extended, dealing area damage to all nearby enemies. This area-of-effect ability provides crowd control through damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (6 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 100% of Attack Power per spin
- Number of Spins: 5 spins over duration
- Total Damage: 500% of Attack Power (per enemy)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per spin)

**Area Effect:**
- Hits all enemies in radius
- Continuous damage while channeling
- Movement speed reduced by 50% while channeling
- Can be interrupted by damage

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual whirling blades
- Area coverage
- Multiple hits per enemy
- Essential area ability
