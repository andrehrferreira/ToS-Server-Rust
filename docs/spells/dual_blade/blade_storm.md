# Blade Storm

## Overview

Blade Storm creates a devastating storm of blades that deals massive area damage to all nearby enemies. This rare area ability provides exceptional crowd control through damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (10 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.5 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 35 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 80 Stamina
**Duration:** 3.0 seconds (continuous storm)

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 120% of Attack Power per second
- Total Damage: 360% of Attack Power (per enemy, over duration)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per tick)

**Area Effect:**
- Hits all enemies in radius
- Continuous damage over duration
- Movement speed reduced by 60% while channeling
- Can be interrupted by damage

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual blade storm
- Massive area coverage
- High total damage potential
- Essential area ability
