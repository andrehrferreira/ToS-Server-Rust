# Precision Cut

## Overview

Precision Cut delivers a single, precise strike with both blades simultaneously, dealing massive damage to a single target. This high-damage ability provides exceptional single-target damage output.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 300% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (250% critical damage)
- Ignores 40% of target's armor

**Additional Effects:**
- High single-target damage
- Visual precision cut
- Essential damage ability
- Core offensive ability
