# Thousand Cuts

## Overview

Thousand Cuts unleashes an overwhelming flurry of rapid strikes, dealing massive damage through countless hits. This rare ability provides the highest burst damage in the dual blade skillset.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** Melee (2 units)
**Cast Time:** Channeled (3.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 40 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 70 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Strikes: 15 strikes
- Damage per Strike: 100% of Attack Power
- Total Damage: 1500% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per strike)

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 80% while channeling
- Each strike every 0.2 seconds

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual thousand cuts
- Overwhelming strike animation
- High burst damage potential
- Essential burst ability
