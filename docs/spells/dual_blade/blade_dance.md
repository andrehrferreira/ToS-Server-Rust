# Blade Dance

## Overview

Blade Dance combines movement and attack, allowing the warrior to strike while moving between targets. This mobility attack ability provides both damage and positioning advantage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target (Movement)
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 10 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 180% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Movement:**
- Dashes to target
- Strikes during dash
- Can continue movement after strike
- Visual blade dance effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Mobility and damage combo
- Essential mobility ability
- High utility ability
- Core movement ability
