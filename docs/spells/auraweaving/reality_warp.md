# Reality Warp

## Overview

Reality Warp is the ultimate auraweaving ability, manipulating space itself to provide massive group teleportation, area buffs, and enemy debuffs. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of space manipulation mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 40 units
**Area Radius:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 4.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 300 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 200 Mana

## Effects

**Group Teleportation:**
- Teleports all allies in 15 unit radius to target location
- Instant teleportation
- Brief invulnerability (0.5 seconds)

**Ally Buff:**
- Damage Increase: +40% for 45 seconds
- Defense Increase: +30% for 45 seconds
- Movement Speed Increase: +50% for 45 seconds

**Enemy Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -40% for 45 seconds
- Movement Speed Reduction: -50% for 45 seconds
- Applies to all enemies in target area

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 95% while channeling
- Ultimate space manipulation
- Maximum group utility
