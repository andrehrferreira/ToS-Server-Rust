# Speed Boost

## Overview

Speed Boost enhances movement speed for the caster and nearby allies, providing mobility advantage in combat. This mobility buff is essential for positioning and escape.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (10 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Movement Speed Increase: +35%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled
- Visual speed effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Group-wide mobility
- Essential for positioning
- High utility ability
- Support mobility
