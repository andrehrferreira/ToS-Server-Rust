# Mass Teleport

## Overview

Mass Teleport instantly teleports the caster and all nearby allies to a target location, providing group mobility. This rare ability provides exceptional group positioning advantage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 25 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 80 Mana

## Effects

**Teleportation:**
- Teleports caster and all allies in 8 unit radius
- Instant teleportation
- Brief invulnerability (0.3 seconds)
- Visual group teleport effect

**Additional Effects:**
