# Aura Nova

## Overview

Aura Nova unleashes a burst of aura energy that buffs all nearby allies and debuffs all nearby enemies simultaneously. This rare ability provides both offensive and defensive benefits in a single cast.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies and Enemies
**Range:** Self (15 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 70 Mana

## Effects

**Ally Buff:**
- Damage Increase: +20%
- Defense Increase: +15%
- Duration: 30 seconds
- Applies to all allies

**Enemy Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -20%
- Movement Speed Reduction: -25%
- Duration: 30 seconds
- Applies to all enemies

**Additional Effects:**
- Dual effect ability
- Group support and control
- High utility ability
- Essential for group content
