# Aura Mastery

## Overview

Aura Mastery enhances all aura effects, increasing their effectiveness and duration. This rare buff ability maximizes the power of all auraweaving abilities.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 90 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Aura Effectiveness: +50%
- Aura Duration: +50%
- Applies to all auras
- Cannot be dispelled

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual mastery effect
- Enhanced aura visuals
- Maximum aura potential
- Essential for aura builds
