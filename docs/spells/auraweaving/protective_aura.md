# Protective Aura

## Overview

Protective Aura creates a defensive aura around the caster that reduces incoming damage for all nearby allies. This group defensive ability is essential for party support and survivability.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 45 seconds

## Effects

**Defense:**
- Damage Reduction: +20%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled
- Visual aura effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Group-wide protection
- Continuous aura effect
- Essential for group content
- High utility ability
