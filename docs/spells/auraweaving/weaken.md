# Weaken

## Overview

Weaken applies a debuff to an enemy that reduces their damage output and effectiveness. This debuff ability provides crowd control and reduces enemy threat.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Debuff:**
- Damage Dealt Reduction: -25%
- Attack Speed Reduction: -20%
- Cannot be dispelled by target
- Visual debuff effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Reduces enemy effectiveness
- Essential for crowd control
- High utility ability
- Support debuff
