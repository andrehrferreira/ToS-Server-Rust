# Teleport

## Overview

Teleport instantly moves the caster to a target location within range, providing exceptional mobility for positioning, escape, and tactical advantage. This fundamental mobility spell is essential for auraweaving builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana

## Effects

**Movement:**
- Instant teleportation to target location
- No travel time
- Cannot be interrupted during cast
- Line of sight required

**Additional Effects:**
- Brief invulnerability during teleport (0.2 seconds)
- Can teleport through some obstacles
- Visual effect at origin and destination

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Aura gathering animation
- Space distortion effect
- Teleport preparation

**Teleportation:**
- Fade out at origin
- Portal/rift effect
- Fade in at destination
- Aura particles

**Sound Effects:**
- Aura gathering sound
- Teleport whoosh
- Arrival sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Escaping dangerous situations
- Repositioning in combat
- Closing distance to enemies
- Avoiding area attacks

**Combat Tips:**
- Use to escape melee range
- Teleport behind enemies for advantage
- Use to dodge area attacks
- Combine with other mobility spells

**Positioning:**
- Teleport to high ground
- Use terrain to advantage
- Teleport to allies for support
- Avoid teleporting into danger

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base range (20 units), 15s cooldown
**Level 2:** +2 units range, -1s cooldown
**Level 3:** +4 units range, -2s cooldown
**Level 4:** +6 units range, -3s cooldown
**Level 5:** +8 units range, -4s cooldown, +0.1s invulnerability
