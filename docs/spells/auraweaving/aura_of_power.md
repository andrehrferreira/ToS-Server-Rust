# Aura of Power

## Overview

Aura of Power creates an offensive aura around the caster that increases damage dealt for all nearby allies. This group offensive buff is essential for party DPS enhancement.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 45 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Damage Increase: +25%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Cannot be dispelled
- Visual power aura effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Group-wide damage increase
- Continuous aura effect
- Essential for group content
- High DPS utility
