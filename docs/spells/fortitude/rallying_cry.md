# Rallying Cry

## Overview

Rallying Cry inspires nearby allies, increasing their defense and providing a brief damage reduction buff. This group defensive ability is essential for party protection.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Allies
**Range:** Self (12 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Defense Increase: +25%
- Damage Reduction: +15%
- Applies to all allies in radius
- Visual rally effect

**Additional Effects:**
