# Shield Wall

## Overview

Shield Wall is a defensive stance that significantly reduces incoming damage at the cost of movement speed. This fundamental defensive ability is essential for tank builds, allowing warriors to absorb damage and protect allies.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 5 seconds (toggle)
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina (activation)
**Duration:** Toggle (until deactivated)

## Effects

**Defense:**
- Damage Reduction: 40%
- Applies to all damage types
- Stacks with other defensive abilities

**Movement:**
- Movement Speed: -30%
- Cannot sprint while active
- Can still attack and use abilities

**Additional Effects:**
- Increased threat generation (+50%)
- Visual shield effect
- Can be toggled on/off

## Visual Effects

**Activation:**
- Shield materialization
- Defensive stance animation
- Aura effect

**Active State:**
- Shield barrier visual
- Defensive particles
- Stance animation

**Deactivation:**
- Shield fade out
- Return to normal stance
- Effect dissipation

**Sound Effects:**
- Shield activation sound
- Defensive impact sounds
- Deactivation sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Tanking damage for party
- Surviving heavy damage phases
- Protecting allies
- Boss encounters

**Combat Tips:**
- Activate before taking heavy damage
- Use with Taunt for threat generation
- Combine with other defensive abilities
- Toggle off when mobility needed

**Tanking Synergy:**
- Combines with Taunt for threat control
- Works with Iron Skin for resistance stacking
- Use with Guardian's Aegis for maximum defense

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base reduction (40%), -30% movement speed
**Level 2:** +5% reduction, -25% movement speed penalty
**Level 3:** +10% reduction, -20% movement speed penalty
**Level 4:** +15% reduction, -15% movement speed penalty
**Level 5:** +20% reduction, -10% movement speed penalty, +25% threat generation
