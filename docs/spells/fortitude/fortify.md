# Fortify

## Overview

Fortify temporarily increases maximum health, providing additional survivability for difficult encounters. This defensive ability gives tanks extra health buffer for heavy damage phases.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Health Increase:**
- Maximum Health Increase: +50%
