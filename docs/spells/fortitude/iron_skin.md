# Iron Skin

## Overview

Iron Skin hardens the warrior's skin, significantly increasing physical resistance and reducing incoming physical damage. This defensive buff is essential for tank survivability.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana
**Duration:** 60 seconds

## Effects

**Defense:**
- Physical Resistance: +40%
- Damage Reduction: +25% (physical only)
- Applies to all physical damage
- Visual iron skin effect

**Additional Effects:**
- High physical defense
- Essential for tank builds
- Stacks with other defenses
- Core defensive ability
