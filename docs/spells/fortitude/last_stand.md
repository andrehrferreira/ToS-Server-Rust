# Last Stand

## Overview

Last Stand provides massive damage reduction when health drops critically low, allowing the warrior to survive and continue fighting. This rare survival ability is essential for tank builds.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant (passive trigger)
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 0 Mana (passive)
**Duration:** 10 seconds
**Trigger:** Health below 20%
