# Taunt

## Overview

Taunt forces an enemy to focus their attacks on the caster, generating massive threat and protecting allies. This fundamental tank ability is essential for threat management and party protection.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina

## Effects

**Threat Generation:**
- Threat Increase: +500%
- Forces enemy to attack caster
- Cannot be resisted
- Visual taunt effect
