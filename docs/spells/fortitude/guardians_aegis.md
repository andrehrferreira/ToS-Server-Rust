# Guardian's Aegis

## Overview

Guardian's Aegis creates an impenetrable protective barrier that absorbs massive amounts of damage. This rare defensive ability provides exceptional survivability for tanks and priority targets.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Self or Ally
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 90 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Shield:**
- Damage Absorption: 500% of Magic Power
- Absorbs all damage types
- Shield breaks when absorption depleted
- Visual aegis effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Massive damage absorption
- Essential for tank protection
- High survivability increase
- Maximum defense ability
