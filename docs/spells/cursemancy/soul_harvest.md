# Soul Harvest

## Overview

Soul Harvest channels dark energy to steal life force from the target, dealing damage while restoring health. This rare life-steal ability provides both offense and survivability.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.5 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 120% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 300% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Life Steal:**
- Health Restored: 40% of damage dealt
- Can overheal (temporary health)
- Visual life steal effect
- High survivability

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual soul harvest
- Life steal effect
- Essential survivability ability
- High utility ability
