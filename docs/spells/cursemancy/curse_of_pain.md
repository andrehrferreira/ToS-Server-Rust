# Curse of Pain

## Overview

Curse of Pain applies a debilitating curse that deals continuous dark damage over time. This damage-over-time ability provides sustained damage and curse application.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 10 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 10% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 200% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can stack with other curses

**Additional Effects:**
- Continuous damage over time
- Visual curse effect
- Essential DoT ability
- Core curse ability

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**

**Active State:**

**Sound Effects:**

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Damage over time
- Sustained damage
- Curse application

**Combat Tips:**

- Use for sustained damage
