# Soul Burn

## Overview

Soul Burn channels dark energy to burn both the target's health and mana, dealing damage while draining resources. This resource-drain ability provides both offense and resource management.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 90% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 180% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Drain:**
- Mana Drain: 15% of damage dealt
- Health Drain: 10% of damage dealt
- Resource management ability

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual soul burn
- Resource drain effect
- Essential resource ability
- High utility ability
