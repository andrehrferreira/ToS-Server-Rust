# Abyssal Strike

## Overview

Abyssal Strike channels abyssal energy into a devastating strike that deals massive dark damage. This high-damage ability provides exceptional single-target damage output.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 45 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 280% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Ignores 25% of target's magic resistance

**Additional Effects:**
- High single-target damage
- Visual abyssal strike
- Essential damage ability
- Core offensive ability
