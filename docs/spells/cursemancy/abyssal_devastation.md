# Abyssal Devastation

## Overview

Abyssal Devastation is the ultimate cursemancy ability, unleashing the full power of the abyss to deal catastrophic area dark damage. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of dark magic mastery.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 30 units
**Area Radius:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** 4.5 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 180 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 200 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 500% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Massive area coverage

**Conditions:**
- Curse Application: 100% chance
- Curse Duration: 20 seconds
- Multiple curses possible
- Visual devastation effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 95% while channeling
- Ultimate area dark damage
- Maximum devastation potential
