# Dark Bolt

## Overview

Dark Bolt launches a bolt of dark energy at the target, dealing dark damage and potentially applying curse effects. This fundamental dark magic spell provides reliable single-target damage with dark magic properties.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Projectile
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 18 units
**Cast Time:** 1.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 4 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 130% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Conditions:**
- Curse Chance: 25%
- Curse Duration: 6 seconds
- Curse Effect: -10% damage dealt, +10% damage taken

**Additional Effects:**
- Dark energy trail
- Can pierce through enemies (50% damage after first)

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Dark energy gathering
- Abyssal particles
- Release animation

**Projectile:**
- Dark bolt with trail
- Purple/black energy
- Abyssal effects

**Impact:**
- Dark explosion
- Curse visual effect (if applied)
- Energy dissipation

**Sound Effects:**
- Dark energy sound
- Projectile whoosh
- Impact sound
- Curse effect sound

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Single target dark damage
- Applying curses
- Consistent magical DPS
- Dark magic builds

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on enemies weak to dark
- Combine with other curse spells
- Stack curses for maximum effect
- Use for damage over time setup

**Curse Synergy:**
- Combines with other curse spells
- Stacks with Curse Mastery
- Works with Cursed Mark for vulnerability

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base damage (130% Magic Power), 25% curse chance
**Level 2:** +10% damage, +5% curse chance
**Level 3:** +20% damage, +10% curse chance
**Level 4:** +30% damage, +15% curse chance
**Level 5:** +40% damage, +20% curse chance, +1s curse duration
