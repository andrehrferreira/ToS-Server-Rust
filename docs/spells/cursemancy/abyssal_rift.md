# Abyssal Rift

## Overview

Abyssal Rift tears open a rift to the abyss, dealing massive area dark damage to all enemies in a large area. This rare ability provides exceptional area damage output.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 25 units
**Area Radius:** 10 units
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 50 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 110 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 350% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Large area coverage

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual abyssal rift

- High total damage potential
- Essential area ability
