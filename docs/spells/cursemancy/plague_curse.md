# Plague Curse

## Overview

Plague Curse applies a spreading curse that deals damage over time and can spread to nearby enemies. This rare ability provides area damage through curse spreading.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target (Spreading)
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 2.0 seconds
**Cooldown:** 35 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 70 Mana
**Duration:** 25 seconds

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 12% of Magic Power per second
- Total Damage: 300% of Magic Power (over duration)
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can spread to nearby enemies

**Spreading:**
- Spread Range: 5 units
- Spread Chance: 30% per second
- Can spread multiple times
- Visual plague effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Spreading curse effect
- Area damage potential
- Essential spreading ability
- High utility ability
