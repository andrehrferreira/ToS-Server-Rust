# Void Blast

## Overview

Void Blast unleashes a burst of void energy that deals area dark damage to all nearby enemies. This area-of-effect ability provides crowd control through damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Area Centered
**Target Type:** Enemies
**Range:** Self (8 unit radius)
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 14 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Dark Damage: 150% of Magic Power
- Damage Type: Dark
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Hits all enemies in radius
