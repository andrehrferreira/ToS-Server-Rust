# Cursed Mark

## Overview

Cursed Mark places a mark of vulnerability on the target, increasing all damage taken from dark magic sources. This debuff ability enhances the effectiveness of all curse spells.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 22 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 25 Mana
**Duration:** 30 seconds

## Effects

**Debuff:**
- Dark Damage Taken Increase: +40%
- Applies to all dark magic damage
- Cannot be dispelled by target
- Visual cursed mark

**Additional Effects:**
- Enhances all curse damage
- Essential for curse builds
- High utility ability
- Core debuff ability
