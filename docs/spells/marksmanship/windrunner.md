# Windrunner

## Overview

Windrunner channels the power of wind to enhance movement speed and attack speed, allowing the archer to move and attack with exceptional swiftness. This rare mobility buff is essential for kiting and maintaining combat advantage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana
**Duration:** 20 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Movement Speed Increase: +40%
- Attack Speed Increase: +30%
- Can use abilities while moving: Yes
- Applies to all movement and attacks

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual wind effect
- Enhanced mobility
- Cannot be dispelled
- Stacks with equipment bonuses
