# Arrow Storm

## Overview

Arrow Storm unleashes a devastating barrage of arrows over a large area, raining destruction on all enemies within the effect radius. This rare ability provides massive area damage, making it one of the most powerful area-of-effect attacks in the marksmanship skillset.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 25 units
**Area Radius:** 10 units
**Cast Time:** 2.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 80 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 100% of Attack Power per arrow
- Number of Arrows: 20-30 (random)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per arrow)
- Damage Duration: 3 seconds (arrows fall over time)

**Area Effect:**
- Large area coverage
- Arrows fall continuously
- Multiple hits per enemy possible
- Visual area indicator

**Additional Effects:**
- Area denial effect
- Continuous damage over duration
- High total damage potential
- Impressive visual effect

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Bow raised high
- Massive arrow preparation
- Skyward release

**Projectile:**
- Massive arrow barrage
- Arcing arrows from sky
- Continuous rain effect

**Area Effect:**
- Large impact area
- Multiple impact effects
- Dust and debris
- Arrow sticking in ground

**Sound Effects:**
- Massive draw sound
- Arrow release sound
- Continuous impact sounds
- Area effect sounds

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Massive area damage
- Clearing large groups
- Area denial
- Boss encounters with adds

**Combat Tips:**
- Use on grouped enemies
- Target center of formations
- Use after crowd control
- Combine with traps for control

**Area Synergy:**
- Combines with Volley
- Works with area control spells
- Effective with team coordination
- Maximum damage on groups

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base (20-30 arrows, 100% each)
**Level 2:** +3 arrows, +5% damage per arrow
**Level 3:** +6 arrows, +10% damage per arrow
**Level 4:** +9 arrows, +15% damage per arrow
**Level 5:** +12 arrows, +20% damage per arrow, +1 unit radius
