# Volley

## Overview

Volley unleashes a barrage of arrows in a targeted area, raining down destruction on all enemies within the effect radius. This spell excels at area damage and hitting multiple targets simultaneously.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 20 units
**Area Radius:** 6 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 35 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 80% of Attack Power per arrow
- Number of Arrows: 8-12 (random)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per arrow)

**Additional Effects:**
- Arrows hit all enemies in area
- Damage distributed across targets
- Visual indicator shows impact area

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Bow raised skyward animation
- Arrow nocking animation
- Release animation

**Projectile:**
- Multiple arrows arcing through air
- Trail effects on arrows
- Impact effects on ground

**Area Effect:**
- Ground impact markers
- Dust and debris effects
- Arrow sticking in ground

**Sound Effects:**
- Multiple arrow release sounds
- Whistling arrow sounds
- Impact sounds (multiple)

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Area damage on grouped enemies
- Clearing multiple weak enemies
- Area denial
- Damage over time in area

**Combat Tips:**
- Target center of enemy groups
- Use after crowd control to maximize hits
- Combine with traps for area control
- Use from elevation for better visibility

**Positioning:**
- Cast from safe distance
- Aim for enemy clusters
- Consider enemy movement patterns
- Use terrain for advantage

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base damage (80% per arrow, 8-12 arrows)
**Level 2:** +1 arrow (9-13 arrows)
**Level 3:** +2 arrows, +5% damage (10-14 arrows, 85% damage)
**Level 4:** +3 arrows, +10% damage (11-15 arrows, 90% damage)
**Level 5:** +4 arrows, +15% damage (12-16 arrows, 95% damage)
