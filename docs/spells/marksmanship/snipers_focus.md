# Sniper's Focus

## Overview

Sniper's Focus channels immense precision into a single, devastating shot that deals massive damage to the target. This rare ability provides the highest single-target damage in the marksmanship skillset, perfect for eliminating priority targets.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 35 units
**Cast Time:** 3.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 30 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 60 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 400% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (200% critical damage)
- Ignores 50% of target's armor

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 80% while channeling
- Visual focus indicator

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot miss (100% accuracy)
- Pierces through obstacles (if line of sight established)
- High critical strike chance (+50%)
- Visual targeting line

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Eliminating priority targets
- Boss damage phases
- High single-target damage
- Finishing off enemies

**Combat Tips:**
- Use when safe from interruption
- Combine with Mark Target for maximum damage
- Use Eagle Eye before casting
