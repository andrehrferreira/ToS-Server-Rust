# Rain of Death

## Overview

Rain of Death is the ultimate marksmanship ability, calling down an apocalyptic storm of arrows that devastates a massive area. This ancient spell represents the pinnacle of ranged combat mastery, dealing catastrophic damage to all enemies caught within its radius.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Ancient (Orange)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 30 units
**Area Radius:** 15 units
**Cast Time:** 4.0 seconds (channeled)
**Cooldown:** 120 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 150 Mana
**Duration:** 5 seconds (continuous rain)

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 120% of Attack Power per arrow
- Number of Arrows: 50-70 (random, over duration)
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per arrow)
- Total Potential Damage: 6000-8400% Attack Power (maximum)

**Area Effect:**
- Massive area coverage
- Continuous arrow rain
- Multiple hits per enemy
- Area denial effect

**Additional Effects:**
- Cannot be interrupted once channeling starts
- Movement speed reduced by 90% while channeling
- Visual area indicator
- Impressive visual spectacle
