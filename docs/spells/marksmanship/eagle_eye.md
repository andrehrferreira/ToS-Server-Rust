# Eagle Eye

## Overview

Eagle Eye enhances the archer's vision and accuracy, significantly increasing attack range and critical strike chance. This self-buff ability improves the effectiveness of all ranged attacks for its duration.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Target Self
**Target Type:** Self
**Range:** Self
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 60 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 30 Mana
**Duration:** 45 seconds

## Effects

**Buff:**
- Attack Range Increase: +30%
- Critical Strike Chance: +25%
- Accuracy Increase: +20%
- Applies to all ranged attacks

**Additional Effects:**
- Visual eagle eye effect
- Enhanced vision range
- Cannot be dispelled
- Stacks with equipment bonuses
