# Trap Arrow

## Overview

Trap Arrow places a hidden trap on the ground that triggers when enemies step on it, dealing damage and applying conditions. This rare ability provides area control and damage over time, perfect for strategic positioning and area denial.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Rare (Blue)
**Spell Type:** Target Area
**Target Type:** Ground Location
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** 1.5 seconds
**Cooldown:** 25 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 50 Mana
**Trap Duration:** 60 seconds
**Trigger Radius:** 3 units

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 150% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes
- Triggers on enemy contact

**Conditions:**
- Slow Duration: 5 seconds
- Slow Effect: -40% movement speed
- Bleed Chance: 60%
- Bleed Duration: 8 seconds
- Bleed Damage: 5% of Attack Power per second

**Trap Properties:**
- Invisible to enemies (until triggered)
- Can place multiple traps
- Maximum 3 traps active simultaneously
- Visual indicator for caster only

## Usage Notes

- Chokepoint defense

**Combat Tips:**
