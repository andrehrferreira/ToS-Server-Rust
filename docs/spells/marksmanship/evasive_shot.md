# Evasive Shot

## Overview

Evasive Shot combines mobility with offense, allowing the archer to leap backward while firing an arrow at the target. This ability provides both damage and positioning advantage, essential for kiting and maintaining distance.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 20 units
**Cast Time:** Instant
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 20 Stamina

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Physical Damage: 110% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes

**Movement:**
- Leaps backward 8 units
- Maintains facing toward target
- Cannot be interrupted during leap
- Brief invulnerability during leap (0.3 seconds)

**Additional Effects:**
- Arrow fires during leap
- Creates distance from enemy
- Can be used while moving

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Creating distance from enemies
- Kiting melee enemies
- Escaping dangerous situations
- Maintaining range advantage

**Combat Tips:**
- Use when enemy closes distance
- Combine with other mobility spells
- Use to escape area attacks
- Maintain optimal range

**Mobility Synergy:**
- Combines with Windrunner for speed
- Works with other kiting abilities
- Essential for ranged combat survival
