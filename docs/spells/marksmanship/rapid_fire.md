# Rapid Fire

## Overview

Rapid Fire unleashes a flurry of arrows in quick succession, dealing multiple hits to the target. This burst damage ability provides high damage output over a short period, perfect for finishing off enemies or dealing burst damage.

## Spell Details

**Rarity:** Common (Green)
**Spell Type:** Single Target
**Target Type:** Enemy
**Range:** 22 units
**Cast Time:** Channeled (2.0 seconds)
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Resource Cost:** 40 Mana

## Effects

**Damage:**
- Arrows Fired: 5 arrows
- Damage per Arrow: 80% of Attack Power
- Total Damage: 400% of Attack Power
- Damage Type: Physical
- Can Critical Hit: Yes (per arrow)

**Channeling:**
- Must channel for full duration
- Can be interrupted by damage
- Movement speed reduced by 50% while channeling
- Each arrow fires every 0.4 seconds

**Additional Effects:**
- All arrows target same enemy
- Visual rapid fire effect
- High burst damage potential

## Visual Effects

**Casting:**
- Rapid draw animation
- Continuous arrow release
- Channeling effect

**Projectiles:**
- Multiple arrows in sequence
- Rapid trail effects
- Continuous impact

**Sound Effects:**
- Rapid draw sounds
- Multiple arrow releases
- Continuous impact sounds

## Usage Notes

**Best Used For:**
- Burst damage on single target
- Finishing off enemies
- High damage output
- Channeled damage ability

**Combat Tips:**
- Use when safe from interruption
- Combine with Mark Target for increased damage
- Use on stationary targets
- Position safely before channeling

**Burst Synergy:**
- Combines with Mark Target
- Works with damage buffs
- High critical strike potential
- Effective for burst builds

## Upgrades

**Level 1:** Base (5 arrows, 80% each)
**Level 2:** +1 arrow, +5% damage per arrow
**Level 3:** +2 arrows, +10% damage per arrow
**Level 4:** +3 arrows, +15% damage per arrow
**Level 5:** +4 arrows, +20% damage per arrow, -0.5s channel time
